Laws and policies

Our building operates under a set of laws and policies that every individual residing in the premise must follow.
These laws are meant to keep the community safe and ensure that the environment is comfortable.

The two main policies of our building are -
1) Noise policy: We have a set of rules that help us minimize the amount of noise that may cause inconvenience to other residents.

2) Renovation policy: We will do our duty of keeping the residents updated about ongoing renovations, and the residents have to adjust to the renovation schedule.
